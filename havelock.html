HAVELOCK

Farley Havelock is a former ally of Corvo Attano. Once an admiral in the Gristol navy, he was discharged for refusing to sail under the command of the Lord Regent, Hiram Burrows.

In partnership with Treavor Pendleton and Teague Martin, he created the Loyalist Conspiracy with the aim of reinstating the rightful heir to the Empire, Emily Kaldwin, to the throne.

After his plans come to fruition, Havelock takes the mantle of Lord Regent and betrays Corvo thereby becoming the main antagonist for the last third of Dishonored.

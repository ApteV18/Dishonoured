Sokolv & Piero

Piero Joplin and Anotov Sokolv are natural philosopher's, inventor's (whose creations include an anti-plague potion known as Piero's Spiritual Remedy, Corvo's Mask, and later, an advanced Arc Pylon) and a member of the Loyalist Conspiracy who resides above the workshop at the Hound Pits Pub.

Throughout Dishonored, Piero supplies Corvo Attano with ammunition and equipment upgrades, provided Corvo has the required coin to purchase them.

He also designs and constructs Corvo's mask
